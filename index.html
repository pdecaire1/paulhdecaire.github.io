About

My research examines professional financial decision-making in resource allocation through a behavioral lens, using firms and financial markets as natural laboratories. Recent projects investigate:

  1. How strategic interactions in managerial learning games impact investment decisions
  2. The role of heuristics in capital budgeting and valuation
  3. The mental models that shape the decisions of financial market professionals

For more information on these projects, please visit my research page.
